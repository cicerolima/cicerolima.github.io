Biography

Cicero Lima is a Postdoctoral research associate at Sao Paulo School of Economics, Fundação Getulio Vargas, Brazil. His research interest concerns agricultural economics, climate change, land-use and land-use changes. He is working currently in the application of applied computable general equilibrium models (CGE) to cost effectiveness analysis of climate change policy, as also the impacts on land use changes, as well as economic implication of biofuels. Also he works with CGE modeling applied to agricultural economics and trade policy and poverty analysis.
