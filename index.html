Biography

Cicero Lima is a Postdoctoral research associate at Department of Agricultural Economics, Purdue University, USA. His research interest concerns agricultural economics, climate change, land-use and land-use changes. Currently, he is working on the impacts of heat stress on Global agricultural labor, as well as the macroeconomic consequences of enhanced biodiversity and ecosystem services, such as pollination, sea-level raise, water scarcity.
